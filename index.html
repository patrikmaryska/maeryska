Meet Maeryska

Maeryska is a pianist turned musical storyteller, whose journey began at sixteen with his first band, Phoenix in Fire. Since then, he has performed with multiple bands and explored a wide range of genres—from orchestral fantasy to cinematic soundscapes—all shaped by his deep emotional range and love for narrative-driven music.

Maeryska crafts sound like a painter with light—his hands fluent in piano, keyboards, and wind-carved flutes. Each composition emerges from the marrow of real moments, then drifts into realms of myth and wonder, where emotion swells and reason quietly shapes the storm.

With a Master's degree in Software Engineering, he seamlessly blends the logic of technology with the chaos of creativity. Maeryska completely mixes and masters his music, maintaining full artistic control from conception to final production.
