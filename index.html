Meet Maeryska

Maeryska is a pianist turned musical storyteller, whose journey began at sixteen with his first band, Phoenix in Fire. Since then, he has performed with multiple bands and explored a wide range of genres—from orchestral fantasy to cinematic soundscapes—all shaped by his deep emotional range and love for narrative-driven music.

Proficient in piano, keyboards, and a variety of flutes, Maeryska brings his musical visions to life through both live instrumentation and digital composition. His pieces are rooted in real-life experiences, yet rise into mythical and fantastical realms—reflecting a constant clash between raw emotion and a deeply analytical mind.

With a Master’s degree in Software Engineering, he seamlessly blends the logic of technology with the chaos of creativity.
